German Articles — der, die, das

Every German noun has a gender. You must learn the article with the noun. There is no shortcut — but there are patterns!

Masculine
der
Männlich
der Mann (the man)
der Bus (the bus)
der Bruder (the brother)
der Vater (the father)
Feminine
die
Weiblich
die Frau (the woman)
die Schule (the school)
die Mutter (the mother)
die Stadt (the city)
Neuter
das
Neutral
das Kind (the child)
das Haus (the house)
das Buch (the book)
das Auto (the car)
💡 Memory tip: Always learn the article WITH the noun. Don't learn Buch — learn das Buch. Think of the article as part of the word. Use colours: blue for der, purple for die, green for das.
🌍 Gender Patterns (helpful shortcuts)
Usually DER (masc.)
Days: der Montag
Months: der Januar
Seasons: der Sommer
Ending -er: der Lehrer
Ending -ling: der Frühling
Usually DIE (fem.)
Ending -ung: die Wohnung
Ending -heit: die Freiheit
Ending -keit: die Möglichkeit
Ending -tion: die Nation
Ending -schaft: die Freundschaft
Usually DAS (neut.)
Diminutives -chen: das Mädchen
Diminutives -lein: das Fräulein
Infinitives as nouns: das Essen
Many metals: das Gold
Colours: das Blau
