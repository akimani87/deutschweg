Pattern

small number + ending = big number

dreizehn
→
3 → 13
vierzehn
→
4 → 14
dreißig
→
3 → 30
vierzig
→
4 → 40
fünfzehn
→
5 → 15
fünfzig
→
5 → 50

21, 47 — German says it backwards

einundzwanzig
⇄
1 + und + 20
= 21
siebenundvierzig
⇄
7 + und + 40
= 47
German says the small number first — then und — then the tens.
Backwards from English. 100% consistent.
