Before You Begin

German Pronunciation
& Sounds

This module comes before grammar, before vocabulary, before everything. Here is why: if you cannot recognise how German sounds, listening exercises will feel impossible no matter how much vocabulary you know.

Good news first: German is almost perfectly phonetic. Once you learn how each letter sounds, you can read any German word correctly — even words you have never seen before. English is not like this. German rewards you for learning its system.
